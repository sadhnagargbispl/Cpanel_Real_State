About This Project

Sky Residencia Phase I is a premium residential plotting scheme located in the heart of Sector 7, New Delhi. Designed with modern urban living in mind, this gated community offers plots ranging from 5 Marla to 2 Kanal with all state-of-the-art amenities.

The project is NOC-approved and LDA-registered, ensuring complete legal security for buyers. With wide carpeted roads, underground utilities, and a stunning park at the center, Sky Residencia offers the ideal setting for your dream home.
